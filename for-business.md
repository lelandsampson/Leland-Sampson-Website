<h1 align="center">Insurance for Business</h1>

## Why You Need Insurance
While businesses often offer health insurance, those standard plans often don't cover everything life throws at your team. Supplemental insurance is an excellent way to fill those financial gaps, ensuring your employees have an easier time covering unexpected bills while bolstering the overall benefits package you offer. By providing this extra layer of security, you show your staff that you truly care about their well-being.

Offering a robust benefits package is also a powerful tool for recruiting top talent and keeping your best employees happy and loyal. Since employer contributions are completely optional, you can provide these valuable perks without necessarily increasing your budget. Plus, there are practical advantages for everyone: deductions are often taken pre-tax to reduce your company's overall FICA costs and lower your employee's taxable income.

## Coverage Options
There are many different types of insurance available to cover the wide variety of situations your employees might face. Examples of supplemental insurance include:

* **Short-term disability coverage**
* **Accident insurance**
* **Specified life event insurance**
* **Cancer insurance**
* **Hospital indemnity**
* **Juvenile life insurance**
* **Dental insurance**
* **Vision insurance**

We understand that general health insurance can't catch every curveball, which is why these products are designed to handle specific risks. From maintaining healthy smiles with dental coverage to providing critical financial support during a serious illness, these options provide a comprehensive safety net.
