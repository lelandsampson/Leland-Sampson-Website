Presentation Slides

Building on their well-received presentation at the 2023 Conference, Lee and Dave will share additional information on practical ways that IOLTA funders can use new Al tools and other tech in their offices. The presentation will highlight a variety of options, but will pause to go deeper with key tools, sharing specific use cases, and facilitating some audience conversation to get the juices flowing for how ideas can be taken home and put to work, improving efficiency and ease. The goal is for participants to leave with new tools to consider and greater confidence in managing their own technology solutions.
